sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: time spent sorting already sorted numbers is much less than the time spent sorting unsorted numbers

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: time spent sorting the already sorted numbers and the time spent sorting the unsorted numbers is similar and is much less than the time spent by the other two

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: time spent sorting the already sorted numbers is equal to the time spent sorting the unsorted numbers
