Exploring trails

Exploring trails in Belo Horizonte offers a great connection with nature close to the city, especially on the slopes of Serra do Curral and surrounding areas.

Grotão trail

One of the most memorable trails I've taken was the famous Grotão/Aguinhas trail in Serra do Curral. Along the steep stretches, we crossed impressive natural formations and continued through the Jambreiro Forest to Pico do Grotão, returning via the beautiful Aguinhas Trail, passing through the Córrego da Fazenda canyon a route remembered for its diverse landscapes.

Tunnels trail

Another memorable trail was through the tunnels between Sabará and Belo Horizonte. This alternative route runs through old tunnels, often with muddy or flooded sections, offering a unique and challenging experience, combining history and adventure.
